About Randi Gems Ceylon

Randi Gems Ceylon is a premium Sri Lankan gemstone and jewelry brand established in 2023, dedicated to showcasing the timeless beauty of natural Ceylon gems to the world. We specialize in rare sapphires, spinels, garnets, zircon, moonstones, and handcrafted luxury jewelry collections designed with elegance, authenticity, and international quality standards. In addition to fine gemstones and jewelry, Randi Gems Ceylon also offers unique gemstone wellness collections, including Yoga Gem Kits and spiritual lifestyle products inspired by balance, mindfulness, and positive energy. With a strong commitment to ethical sourcing, trusted craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction, we proudly connect Sri Lanka’s rich gemstone heritage with clients across the globe.

Mission

To provide authentic Sri Lankan gemstones and jewelry with elegance, trust and world-class craftsmanship.

Vision

To become a globally respected luxury gemstone brand representing Sri Lankan heritage and excellence.
