About Rhodolite Garnet

Rhodolite Garnets are admired for their attractive raspberry-red and purplish-red colors. These beautiful gemstones combine the brilliance of garnets with elegant shades that are highly sought after in fine jewelry.

Available Varieties

  • Rose Red Rhodolite
